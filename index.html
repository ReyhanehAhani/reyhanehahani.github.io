About

I’m a Master’s student in Computer Engineering at Simon Fraser University, also working as a Big Data Analyst at the SFU Big Data Hub on public-sector projects. I like problems where the hard part isn’t only the model—it’s joining messy sources, validating carefully, and explaining results to people who need to act on them.
